FAQ

Common Questions

How much does an epoxy garage floor cost on Vancouver Island?

Pricing depends on square footage, current slab condition, coating system, and finish options. We provide free on-site assessments and detailed quotes so you know exactly what to expect before work begins.

How long does epoxy floor installation take?

Most residential garage projects take two to three days, including surface preparation, coating application, and cure time. Commercial and specialty systems may require additional time depending on scope and environmental requirements.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Victoria, Langford, Saanich, and communities across Vancouver Island for residential, commercial, and industrial epoxy coating projects.

Do you offer commercial and industrial epoxy flooring?

Yes. We install high-performance epoxy and resin systems for warehouses, restaurants, retail spaces, manufacturing facilities, auto shops, healthcare environments, and more.

Why is surface preparation important for epoxy floors?

Proper mechanical grinding and preparation ensures maximum adhesion and long-term durability. Every Epoxy Custom Coatings project follows proven prep standards, because a premium finish starts with a properly prepared slab.
