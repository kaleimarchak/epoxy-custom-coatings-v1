Coating Systems

Premium Finishes & Materials

We install premium resin and specialty coating systems for commercial, industrial, and certified PMMA applications — each selected for substrate conditions, traffic demands, and operational requirements.

Epoxy

High-build epoxy floor coatings create seamless, chemical-resistant surfaces ideal for warehouses, production floors, commercial kitchens, and industrial facilities across Vancouver Island.

Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA)

As certified PMMA installers through Plasti-Chemie North America, we apply ultra-fast-cure systems that perform in extreme cold — ideal for commercial kitchens, food processing, cold storage, and facilities where downtime must be kept to a minimum.

Polyurethane

Polyurethane topcoats add UV stability, abrasion resistance, and flexibility — extending the life and appearance of epoxy and resin floor systems in demanding environments.

Metallic Epoxy

Metallic epoxy produces stunning, reflective floor finishes with depth and movement — a designer option for showrooms, retail spaces, and custom residential projects.

Flake Systems

Decorative flake broadcasts add colour, texture, and slip resistance while helping conceal minor slab imperfections — a popular choice for garages, shops, and commercial floors.

Microcement Finishes

Microcement delivers thin, seamless cement-based finishes for walls and floors — perfect for modern interiors that call for a clean, contemporary aesthetic.

Decorative Lime Coatings

Natural lime-based coatings provide breathable, decorative finishes for interior and exterior surfaces — blending timeless style with durable protection.

ESD Coatings

Static-dissipative and conductive ESD floor systems safeguard sensitive electronics, support clean environments, and help facilities meet strict operational requirements.
